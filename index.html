Founder · 2025 – Present
EXIF Studio
A hospitality-focused visual content and commercial studio for hotels, villas and tourism brands across the Caribbean.

EXIF started as a question: what would it look like to bring editorial-quality visual content to the kinds of properties I've been working in, built specifically around how those properties sell themselves to guests who expect more than a catalog photo? Answering that question meant building a commercial proposition from zero: defining the market, writing the pricing architecture, running outreach, managing early-stage client conversations and developing a brand identity that could stand next to the properties it serves.

The result is a studio built for boutique luxury. More importantly, it gave me a full-cycle look at B2B sales in the hospitality space, which turns out to be a completely different conversation from selling to a guest standing in front of you at a resort.
